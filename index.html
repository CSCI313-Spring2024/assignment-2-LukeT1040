About Me

Hello, my name is Luke Thompson and I am a computer science major here at NDSU. I was born on 09/19/2003 in Bismarck, North Dakota but quickly moved here to West Fargo a few months before any schooling started. I went to high school at West Fargo Sheyenne High School where I played football, played the saxophone in band, and expressed my interest in programming and the computer science world. After college I hope to pursue a job in either cybersecurity or software engineering. I have major interest in both but still want to dip my toes into both fields during internships so I have a good idea on where to go in the future. I hope you have a great time exploring my website and I hope you have a good day.
